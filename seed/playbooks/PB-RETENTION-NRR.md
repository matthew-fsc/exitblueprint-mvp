# Retention and NRR Program

**Roadmap phase:** Phase 2 (6-18mo)

**Value impact:** NRR sub-score uplift; supports Revenue Quality and buyer confidence in forward revenue.

## Tasks

| seq | title | owner_role | offset_days |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Implement cohort revenue tracking to compute NRR annually | ops | 30 |
| 2 | Run churn post-mortems on last 3 years of lost customers | advisor | 60 |
| 3 | Launch retention initiatives on at-risk segments | owner | 120 |

## Why buyers care

A buyer pays for the revenue trajectory embedded in the existing base, not just this year's
sales. A base that holds or expands on its own is worth more than one that must be re-won
every year, because it raises the revenue a buyer can count on without new selling.

## Evidence this produces

- Cohort revenue tracking that computes net revenue retention.
- Churn post-mortems on recent lost customers.
- Retention initiatives targeted at at-risk segments.
