# Growth Engine Program

**Roadmap phase:** Phase 3 (18-36mo)

**Value impact:** Growth score uplift; supports premium-multiple growth story.

## Tasks

| seq | title | owner_role | offset_days |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Implement CRM pipeline with stages, values, close dates | ops | 45 |
| 2 | Define ICP and differentiation; standardize the sales motion | owner | 90 |
| 3 | Productize custom work into standardized offerings where possible | owner | 180 |
| 4 | Report pipeline coverage ratio monthly; target 3x annual revenue | advisor | 210 |

## Why buyers care

Buyers pay for growth they can count on, not growth that depends on the owner's personal
effort. A measured pipeline with coverage against a target, repeatable delivery, and a
defensible reason customers choose you turn a growth story into a higher multiple; growth
asserted without a pipeline reads as hope.

## Evidence this produces

- A CRM pipeline with stages, values, and close dates.
- A defined ideal-customer profile and differentiation, with a standardized sales motion.
- Productized offerings and a monthly pipeline-coverage ratio.
