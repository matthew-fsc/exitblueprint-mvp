# Clean Books Program

**Roadmap phase:** Phase 1 (0-6mo)

**Value impact:** EBITDA quality improvement; reduces retrade risk; Financial Integrity score uplift.

## Tasks

| seq | title | owner_role | offset_days |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Engage outside CPA; define path to reviewed statements | owner | 14 |
| 2 | Implement monthly bank reconciliation discipline | cpa | 30 |
| 3 | Build cash-to-accrual bridge or convert to accrual basis | cpa | 60 |
| 4 | Produce full three-statement package (P&L, BS, Cash Flow) and reconcile | cpa | 90 |
| 5 | Assemble 36-month financial history package | advisor | 120 |

## Why buyers care

Buyers distrust financials that are not reconciled on a regular cadence, and they
underwrite recast (adjusted) EBITDA — the dominant valuation method in the lower middle
market. Accrual-basis, reconciled, three-statement books let diligence start from a
credible base instead of a rebuild.

## Evidence this produces

- Monthly bank reconciliations on a repeatable close.
- A cash-to-accrual bridge (or a converted accrual basis).
- A complete three-statement package and a 36-month history.
