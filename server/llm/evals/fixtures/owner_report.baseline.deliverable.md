## What to fix first

This was flagged as a critical priority. On its own this can stall a deal or force a real price cut, so it comes first. Financial Hygiene scored 52, the clearest drag on the overall score. What the fix looks like: move to accrual-basis statements and stand up a monthly close so the numbers are diligence-ready before a buyer ever asks.

### Customer Concentration

This was flagged as a high priority. Buyers weight this heavily in diligence, so resolving it protects both price and certainty. What the fix looks like: broaden the account base and work toward multi-year agreements with your largest customers.
